Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

byJean-Auguste-Dominique-Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Irgres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of thr Riviere family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions:100x70 cm
Home: Musèe du Louve,Paris
